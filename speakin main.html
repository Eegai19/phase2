Speaking Module Instructions

The speaking test consists of 20 questions. Each question allows 10 seconds to respond. You must wait for the full 10 seconds to pass before moving on to the next question. Listen to each question carefully and answer clearly. Once the timer ends, you will be able to click "Next" to proceed to the following question. You can only respond once, and there will be no second chance to re-record your answer. Make sure to understand the question properly before responding.
